Collector Tips & Care Ideas

These suggestions are simple ideas for keeping Jellycat plushies clean, cozy, and nicely displayed. They are not official instructions from the brand, but examples for this project.

Care Tips

  • Use spot cleaning with a damp cloth and mild soap for small stains.
  • Allow plushies to air-dry fully; avoid dryers when possible.
  • Keep them away from strong sunlight to reduce fabric fading.
  • Rotate which plushies children sleep with so fluff and seams last longer.

Display Ideas

  • Arrange by color or theme to create a more organized shelf.
  • Use low shelves for “everyday cuddle” plushies and higher shelves for rare ones.
  • Place a few plushies in baskets or crates for a cozy, playful look.
  • Dust shelves regularly using a soft cloth or gentle vacuum attachment.
