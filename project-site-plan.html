Site Plan: CuddleCloud – Jellycat Wishlist & Collector Hub

This document outlines the plan for the WDD 231 Individual Project.

Site Name

CuddleCloud – Jellycat Wishlist & Collector Hub

The name “CuddleCloud” reflects the softness of Jellycat plushies and the idea of keeping “wishlist dreams in the cloud.” It is friendly, memorable, and perfectly fits the topic of a collector-focused website.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to:

  • Provide Jellycat fans with curated plush information and categories.
  • Allow users to create and manage a wishlist with interactive UI features.
  • Help collectors track which Jellycats they own.
  • Share care, cleaning, and display tips for collectors.

Scenarios

  • “Which Jellycats are currently on my wishlist, and how can I organize them?”
  • “How do I track which Jellycats I already own so I don’t buy duplicates?”
  • “How do I prioritize which Jellycats to buy next?”
  • “How do I clean and care for my Jellycat plushies?”
